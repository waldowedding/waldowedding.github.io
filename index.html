Our Story

High School Sweethearts

  • 2005

    The early days

    Dillon and Marissa met as freshmen in World History class at Saint Thomas Aquinas High School when they were assigned to sit next to each other. They quickly realized they had a lot in common with their constant joking, goofy personalities, and fun loving spirits. After a group outing at a movie theater, Marissa was the last one to get picked up (because her parents forgot her) but Dillon stayed behind to make sure she got home safely. It was then that Marissa knew she had found a great friend in Dillon.

  • 2007

    The start of something special

    After building a strong friendship over three years, Dillon asked Marissa to Prom their Junior year of high school. This was also when Dillon felt something special with Marissa and asked her to be his girlfriend.

  • 2009-2014

    Off to College

    Dillon and Marissa joined each other at the University of Kansas. They enjoyed their time in Lawrence, Kansas together for 2 years. Marissa then moved to Kansas City after being accepted into the KU School of Nursing. Soon after Dillon transferred to Baker University where he pursued his interest in Computer Science. They would finally be back in the same city when they moved into an apartment in Lenexa, KS.

  • 2014-2015

    Home Sweet Home

    During their time in the apartment, a special furry friend named Hank was adopted into their lives. He gave them a lot of patience, joy, and understanding. After saving up for a year while living in the apartment, they finally found their dream home. They moved into their first home in April of 2015 and have enjoyed all the tasks and challenges that come with being homeowners.

  • 2015

    He asked; She said, "YES!"

    On Marissa's 25th birthday, Dillon surprised her with a weekend trip to Chicago. They jetted off for the Windy City bright and early December 5th, where they had a fun filled day shopping on Michigan Avenue and exploring the sights. Later that night they walked to Millennium Park to see the 'bean', Marissa's favorite attraction in Chicago. On the way there, the couple stopped in front of a giant christmas tree for a photo-op. Dillon asked a passer-by to take their picture. He then got down on one knee and asked Marissa to marry him.

    The adventure continued when Dillon took Marissa to dinner at Chicago Chop House where she was yet again surprised by both their parents waiting for them at their table. It was truly the perfect proposal and the best way to express the true love that Dillon and Marissa share.
